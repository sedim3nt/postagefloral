Postage Floral

An ode to slow and intentional forms of affection

- like snail mail and flowers.

Est. 2023 - Boulder, CO

Our florals are playful and sincere.

Our style balances a lifelong love of nature’s creative beauty and a devotion to color: One part sparkler and one part slow-burning candles. We love exploring the way joy conveys itself in unexpected bursts - and if we’re lucky, lingers.

We are committed to creating unique florals that help capture your most tender moments in an unforgettable flourish.
